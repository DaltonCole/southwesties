## COVID Restrictions

To ensure the safety of all of our dancers we will abide by the current New
Mexico state gathering restrictions. Starting on July 1st, there will no longer
be occupancy restrictions so we can operate at full capacity, however the 
guidelines still require masks for unvaccinated individuals in indoor spaces.
As such, we will be requiring all unvaccinated individuals to wear a mask
during the duration of the dance. All masks must be worn properly, in which the
mask covers both the mouth and the nose. Masks must not contain noticeable 
holes or vents. If a SouthWesties ABQ official or volunteer determines that
the mask does not meet our standards, we will provide a replacement, free of
charge, that the violator can wear.

Any unvaccinated person that takes off their mask and refuses to put it on
after instructed to do so by a SouthWesties ABQ official or volunteer will be
asked to leave and not return for the duration of the event. They are welcome
to participate in any other SouthWesties ABQ events if they abide by the
restrictions. 

All vaccinated persons are not required to wear masks, as per the new state
ordinance, however we will be checking vaccination status to ensure the health
and safety of all of our patrons. In order to demonstrate that a person is 
vaccinated, a SouthWesties ABQ official or volunteer must be shown either

* a CDC vaccination card (or multiple cards) listing the name of the vaccinated
  individual and the date of both shots (for Moderna or Pfizer) or a single
  vaccination date (for Johnson & Johnson).
* a NMDOH vaccination record listing the name of the individual and the date of 
  both shots (for Moderna or Pfizer) or a single vaccination date (for Johnson 
  & Johnson).
* a high-quality digital copy of either of the above. An elligible "high-quality"
  photo is a photo of sufficient quality to adequately see the the name of the 
  vaccinated individual, the date of both shots (for Moderna or Pfizer) or a 
  single vaccination date (for Johnson & Johnson), and the entire card or cards
  must be visible.

Proof of vaccination only needs to be done once per individual. We will keep
a record so returning attendees do not need to provide proof of vaccination
every time they visit.

Please DO NOT email digital copies of vaccination records to us. We do not
use end-to-end encrypted email, so we do not have the necessary secure infrastructure
for storing digital copies of vaccination records.
