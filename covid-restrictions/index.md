## COVID Restrictions

Updated: April 19, 2021

Abiding by facility requirements of Lloyd Show Dance Center,
all SouthWesties ABQ event held at Lloyd Shaw Dance center require 
proof of vaccination for attendance. Wearing a mask is optional.

In order to demonstrate that a person is 
vaccinated, a SouthWesties ABQ official or volunteer must be shown either

* a CDC vaccination card (or multiple cards) listing the name of the vaccinated
  individual and the date of both shots (for Moderna or Pfizer) or a single
  vaccination date (for Johnson & Johnson).
* a NMDOH vaccination record listing the name of the individual and the date of 
  both shots (for Moderna or Pfizer) or a single vaccination date (for Johnson 
  & Johnson).
* a high-quality digital copy of either of the above. An elligible "high-quality"
  photo is a photo of sufficient quality to adequately see the the name of the 
  vaccinated individual, the date of both shots (for Moderna or Pfizer) or a 
  single vaccination date (for Johnson & Johnson), and the entire card or cards
  must be visible.

Proof of vaccination only needs to be done once per individual. We will keep
a record so returning attendees do not need to provide proof of vaccination
every time they visit.

Please DO NOT email digital copies of vaccination records to us. We do not
use end-to-end encrypted email, so we do not have the necessary secure infrastructure
for storing digital copies of vaccination records.

Thank you for your continued support.
