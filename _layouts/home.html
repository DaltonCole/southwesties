Albuquerque West Coast Swing Dancing

We're Almost Dancing!

June 11, 2021

Understatement of the century: last year sucked.

While we believe that the distance was ultimately for the best, we have missed all of our local dancers immeasurably. But that's about to end. We know more and more dance communities have been opening their doors and starting back up, but we're not quite able to open up just yet. But we're working hard to get our weekly dances back up and running.

One impediment is that our old studio is undergoing renovations and repairs right now. We don't have an official word as to when those are going to be done, but nobody wants to dance in the ABQ summer with no air-conditioning. So we're happy the repairs are being made. We're looking for a temporary venue while we wait.

More importantly, we want to make sure that when we open we can do our absolute best to ensure everyone's safety. This means following all the most recent state and federal guidelines regarding indoor, in-person, close-contact gatherings. We want to make sure everyone is healthy and able to come back week after week. That means we have some decisions to make about what requirements we will have when we open up. With the regular controversy surrounding the current state of the pandemic, we know our decisions will not make everyone happy; quite the contrary. Once we have settled on how we believe we can best proceed, we'll let you kow.

Thanks so much for bearing with us. We'll see you soon.
