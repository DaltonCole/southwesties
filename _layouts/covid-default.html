Because of the continuing COVID-19 pandemic, it is important that we maintain social distance practices to limit the spread of the virus. Social dancing is especially risky considering its prolonged close interaction, including physical contact, with many people over the course of the night. As such, we have cancelled all of our dances moving forward until we believe that having dances will not put our community members at risk.

Thank you all so much for your understanding and support. Stay safe; stay healthy.
