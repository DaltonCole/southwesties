Special Event

July 30 – August 2, 2026

Lizzy Spann Workshop Weekend

📍 Lloyd Shaw Dance Center — 5506 Coal Ave SE, Albuquerque, NM 87108

Join us for a special workshop weekend with guest instructor Lizzy Spann! The weekend features beginner and social dancing, workshops, a guided practice, a competitors' retreat, private lessons, and a board game night.

Private lessons are available Friday and Saturday. To book a slot, email southwestiesabq@gmail.com or text (480) 292-6023. Lizzy's rate is $120 per 45 min, paid directly to her.

Registration for RSVP-required events (guided practice, board game night, and competitors' practice) will open during the week of July 13.

Pricing

  • Thursday social dance — $3 (returning dancers); free for first-timers.
  • Friday guided practice — $20. Student rate: $10.
  • Saturday workshops — $20 each or $50 for all three. Student rates: $15 each or $35 for all three.
  • Sunday competitors' practice — $25.
  • Board game night — Free (RSVP required).
  • Private lessons — $120 per 45 min, paid directly to Lizzy.

* A small surcharge will be added to Venmo transactions with SouthWesties.
