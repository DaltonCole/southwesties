About

SouthWesties ABQ is a small, non-profit organization bringing West Coast Swing to New Mexico.

We were founded with the purpose to establish and maintain a friendly, energetic, and supportive environment where everyone is treated equally and/or equitably. We do not tolerate discrimination on the basis of:

  • race, ethnicity, nation of origin or residence;
  • age, religion, marital status, veteran status;
  • sex, gender, sexual preference;
  • manner of dress, skill level or interest in other styles of dance, or;
  • any other protected classes recognized by the city of Albuquerque in the state of New Mexico, by the state of New Mexico, or by the United States of America.

We are committed to operating events that:

  • provide a safe and welcoming environment for everyone, and;
  • educate community members about West Coast Swing and dancing West Coast Swing socially and competitively.

What is West Coast Swing?

West Coast Swing is a partner dance characterized by its elastic look that can be danced to all kinds of musics and artists: from blues to R&B and contemporary, and Aretha Franklin to Dua Lipa and Ed Sheeran!
