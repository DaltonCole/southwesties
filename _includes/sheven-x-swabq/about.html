ARE YOU READY FOR SHEVEN KEKOOLANI?

SouthWesties ABQ is ecstatic to bring All-Star/Champion Lead Sheven Kekoolani to Albuquerque, New Mexico!

Sheven Kekoolani is a widely-recognized instructor, choreographer, judge, and emcee. He has been competing in West Coast Swing for over 15 years. The breadth of his dance education spans from hip-hop to hula and beyond!
