We at SouthWesties want to make West Coast Swing as affordable for the community as possible.

Students* get half-off listed prices!

  • Thursday Night Beginner Lesson & Social Dance - Free for first-time attendees, $3 for returning dancers
  • Friday Evening Guide Dance Practica (2 Hours) - $20
  • Friday Night Board Game Night - Free, RSVP-Only by emailing us at southwestiesabq@gmail.com
  • Saturday Afternoon Workshops (50 Minutes Each) - $30 for all three, or $15 per individual workshop
  • Sunday Afternoon Competitors' Practica (3 Hours) - $20

* Students are individuals who are 18 years old or younger, or have a valid student ID, or have documents confirming enrollment in a school or university.
