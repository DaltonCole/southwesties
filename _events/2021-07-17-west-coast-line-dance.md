Join Donna Ruane and learn how to dance the popular line dance "Have Fun, Go Mad"!
This line dance is a great way to learn some structured footwork patterns
that you can use to spice up your west coast swing dancing. This will occur
before the SSNM Saturday dance.

Admission is $5.

Masks are optional for all vaccinated people. Vaccination status is not checked,
wearing masks is on the honor system.
