title:  "SWABQ Dances at Lloyd Shaw Require Proof of Vaccination"
date:   2021-08-17
---

Lloyd Shaw Dance Center will now be requiring all persons that host and attend
dances at their facility to be fully vaccinated in order to participate. As
such, all dances run by SouthWesties ABQ at Lloyd Shaw will now require proof
of vaccination for **all** participants. Please check our [covid restrictions](covid-restrictions/)
for more details.

As was done previously, proof of vaccination is only required once. All patrons
that have already proven their vaccination status do not need to provide additional
proof.

If this restriction gets lifted we will update you.

Stay safe and stay healthy.
