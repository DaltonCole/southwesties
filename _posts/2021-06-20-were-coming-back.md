So it's been a long hiatus. Obvious understatement about everything that's 
happened in the last year. And while we believe that the distance was ultimately 
for the best, we have missed all of our local dancers immeasurably. But with
an abundance of patience we get to open our doors and see you all again! Our
first dance will be on July 8th! Even though we are coming back, things are 
going to be different. 

First, because
of renovations and repairs at Lloyd Shaw, we will be hosting our Thursday night
dances at a new location while our old facility is inaccessible. The new address
is right nearby, though. Check our our [calendar]({{ "/calendar" | relative_url }})
for the new location.

Next, we will be following New Mexico's guidelines for safely resuming activities
in indoor spaces. Since we are not opening until July, we will be able to open 
at full capacity, but we have new mask restrictions for participating in our 
Thursday dances. For full details see our 
[covid-restrictions page]({{ "/covid-restrictions" | relative_url }}).

Here's the quick-read of the restrictions. Masks are optional for fully
vaccinated individuals. All attendees of the dance must provide proof of vaccination
in order to be allowed to remove their mask. This proof only needs to be done
once. We will accept physical or digital copies of the CDC vaccination cards or 
NMDOH vaccine records. Any digital copy must be clear enough to read the name
and vaccination dates; we must also have a photo of the whole card, no partial
images. Please DO NOT email digital copies of vaccination cards. We do not
use an end-to-end encrypted email service so this is not secure. 

Any unvaccinated individual is welcome to attend but must wear a mask 
properly (over mouth and nose) during the duration of the event. Masks that 
have vents or obvious holes are not acceptable. If we deem a mask unacceptable 
we will provide an alternative that can be worn. Any individual that does
not show proof of vaccination and refuses to wear a mask will be asked to leave.
Anyone asked to leave can return for any future event as long as they follow all
restrictions when attending future events.

We hope to see you all soon!
