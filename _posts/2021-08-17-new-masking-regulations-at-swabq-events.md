Due to the rise in COVID-19 cases from the Delta variant, NM will be 
[temporarily re-implement a statewide requirement that facemasks](https://www.governor.state.nm.us/2021/08/17/new-mexico-to-re-implement-indoor-mask-mandate-vaccinations-required-in-hospitals-congregate-settings/) in indoor spaces.
To ensure the safety of all of our dancers, SouthWesties will be requiring all
attendees of our lessons and social dances to wear masks regardless of
vaccination status.

For more information check out our [covid restrictions](covid-restrictions/)

We appreciate your continued support and will see you on the dance floor.

Stay safe, and stay healthy.
