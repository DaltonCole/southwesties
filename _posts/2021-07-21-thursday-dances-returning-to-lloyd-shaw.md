We're going back to Lloyd Shaw!

Now that the repairs and renovations are done so our Thursday dances are returning 
to our original location: Lloyd Shaw Dance Center 5506 Coal Ave SE, Albuquerque, NM 87108. [Show me on Google!](https://www.google.com/maps/place/Lloyd+Shaw+Dance+Center/@35.0755264,-106.5860617,17z/data=!3m1!4b1!4m5!3m4!1s0x87220b02f19b1667:0x704ddf44b17ed35!8m2!3d35.0756267!4d-106.5838744)
 
Many of our members will remember the days where we used to start in a small 
room and eventually move to a bigger room. Well, good news everyone! We will 
now have access to the large room all night! Gone are the days of fun, cramped, 
overheated dancing. Now we're just going to do the fun part.

We hope to see you there!
