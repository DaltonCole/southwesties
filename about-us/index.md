Sandia Swing is brought to you by the same oddballs from 
[SouthWesties ABQ](https://southwestiesabq.com/). We are a small, non-profit community
organization that works to provide a place for New Mexicans to learn and dance
West Coast Swing. We host weekly dances and occasional weekend workshops. We are very excited,
and honestly a little terrified, to be running such a big event here in The Land
of Enchantment.

If you are interested in our weekly dance, or other west coast swing dances and
events happening in New Mexico, please check out [our calendar](https://southwestiesabq.com/calendar).
We do our best to keep it up to date with all the West Coast Swing events
happening in New Mexico.

Thank you so much for you interest in our event. Our goal is for everyone to 
learn, have a blast social dancing, and to get people excited about West
Caost Swing. If you have any questions or comments, please [contact us]({{ "/contact-us/" | relative_url }}) and we will do our best to address your concerns.
